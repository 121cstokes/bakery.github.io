The History of Tim's Delicious Bakery

How Tim's Delicious Bakery Started How Tim's Delicious Bakery Became Famous
Tim's Delicious Bakery started right here in New Zealand. An ambitious young man named Tim was an amazing Baker since right out of the womb. He was the best in town so he decided to make a bakery to share his skill with the world, and to make a few bucks. Eventually People from all over the world started hearing chatter of this young Tim. People from all different place traveled just to taste the delicious baking of Tim. His business has continued and is still thriving as one of the top bakeries in the world.
